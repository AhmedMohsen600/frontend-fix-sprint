# First Week Checklist

## Day 1: Deploy + Profile Update

- Set `NEXT_PUBLIC_CONTACT_EMAIL`.
- Deploy the site to Vercel.
- Add the site URL to LinkedIn Featured.
- Update LinkedIn headline/about with "React/Next.js Frontend Fix Sprint."

## Day 2: Send 20 Messages

- Send 10 founder DMs.
- Send 10 agency DMs.
- Track every lead in `docs/lead-tracker-template.md`.

## Day 3: Follow Up + Send 20 Messages

- Follow up with Day 2 replies or warm leads.
- Send 20 more targeted messages.
- Save useful replies and objections.

## Day 4: Post Proof/Demo

- Post the dashboard demo.
- Mention the exact offer: 3-5 frontend fixes in 3 days for 250 USD.
- Ask people to send a URL, screenshots, or issue list.

## Day 5: Pitch Agencies

- Message 20 agencies or agency owners.
- Focus on overflow frontend cleanup.
- Offer one trial sprint.

## Day 6: Follow Up

- Follow up with every warm reply.
- Send the proposal when someone has a real issue.
- Ask for repo access only after scope and payment are agreed.

## Day 7: Review Replies And Close

- Mark leads as won, warm, not now, or not a fit.
- Try to close one 250 USD sprint.
- Offer 500 USD/month only after a client shows recurring frontend needs.
